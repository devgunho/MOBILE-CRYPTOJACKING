alt space seattle | welcome

Welcome to ALTSpace, a Community Art Workshop at 23rd & Cherry in Seattle. Creative people of all kinds share this spacious workshop, teaching, experimenting, making and learning. Members can spend time bouncing ideas off one another, hold or attend classes, work away from home and have the space to get even large projects done.

The space offers tons of tools and equipment, 2 restrooms with showers, a public computer, work space and a great environment and people. Memberships range from $75.00 to $300.

If you'd like to see the space, we have a steward onsite on Sundays from 1pm - 4:00pm to give tours. Drop by and check us out!
