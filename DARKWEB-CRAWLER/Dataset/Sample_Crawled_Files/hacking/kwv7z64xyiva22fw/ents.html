What is ENTS?

The Edmonton New Technology Society is a non-profit, member-driven society of makers, technology enthusiasts, hobbyists and friends. ENTS is a makerspace a place to create, build, repair, and work on projects around like minded people.

Opening in August 2009, ENTS has grown into a self-sustaining collective of people with common interests and goals.

How does ENTS work?

EENTS is a co-operative, which means members pay dues, and in turn, ENTS provides a 24/7 workspace, tools and supplies.

As a registered non-profit society in Alberta, ENTS is organized and governed according to bylaws and policies ratified by the membership. These documents can be viewed at DocumENTS.
