“Hackers have become an essential part of our security ecosystem” - Jeff Massimilla, Vice President Global Cybersecurity, General Motors.

Jeff and the General Motors team have been operating their public vulnerability disclosure program with HackerOne for over 2 years. 

They have worked with HackerOne to successfully engage the white hat hacker community, enabling them to reduce risk, and scale security efforts across suppliers and partners. 

They have such success with their HackerOne Response VDP that they are planning on launching a private bug bounty program and other special hacker-powered security efforts.
