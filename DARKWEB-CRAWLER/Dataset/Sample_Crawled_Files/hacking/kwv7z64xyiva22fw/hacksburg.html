About

Hacksburg is Blacksburg's first community makerspace. We're graciously hosted by the Virginia Tech Corporate Research Center at 2200 Kraft Drive, Suite 1475.

To get in, first try going around to the back and knocking on the garage door. Someone will come around to let you in.

Makerspaces (also known as hackerspaces) are community-oriented workshops where people share physical and intellectual resources. Hacksburg is a place to collaborate, a place to learn, and a place to work on projects that you might not be able to work on at home.
