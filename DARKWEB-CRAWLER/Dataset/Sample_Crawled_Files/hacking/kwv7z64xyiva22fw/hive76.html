About Hive76

We are a hackerspace in Kensington, Philadelphia, at 1821 E Hagert St. We are a group of hackers, DIYers, and Makers that tear things apart and put them together in new ways. Please join our announcement list, discussion list, or RSS feed to keep up to date on our exciting projects and events, or just email us at Hive76@gmail.com. And be sure to stop by our open house every Wednesday night!
