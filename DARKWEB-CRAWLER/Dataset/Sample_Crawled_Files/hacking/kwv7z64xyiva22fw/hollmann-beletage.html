Welcome to hollmann beletage hotel

Handmade Viennese Hospitality. Designed for Living.

Design hotel in Vienna's city centre? Boutique Hotel only 2min from St. Stephen's Cathedral?

Some might use the above to describe us. And yet it solely refers to our aesthetical appearance. And as we all know: True beauty lies within.

What we really are, is a place of deceleration in the centre of Vienna. A leisure hotel in the city. A comfy home in an unknown world.

" Here I am human, here they let me be."

J. W. von Goethe.

...as if he already stayed with us, centuries ago.

Free from mainstream, conventions and average.
