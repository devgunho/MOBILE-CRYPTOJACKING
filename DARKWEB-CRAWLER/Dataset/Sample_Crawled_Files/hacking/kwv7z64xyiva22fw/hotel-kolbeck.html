Hotel Kolbeck

Hotel Kolbeck offers pet-friendly accommodation in Vienna.

Each room includes a TV. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom fitted with a shower. Hotel Kolbeck features free WiFi throughout the property.

There is a tour desk at the property.

Belvedere Palace is 1.3 km from Hotel Kolbeck, while Vienna State Opera is 2.3 km from the property. Vienna International Airport is 16 km away.
