Support illutron

illutron is facing new challenges. We have moved to a new location in the harbor, and need your support... See how you can help! here






Interactive art collective

Illutron creates large scale interactive art, intriguing explorations into the realm where art and technology meet.

Manifesto

Free thinking workspace

As a physical framing, the collective owns an old rusty industrial barge with 800 square meters of space. The barge has its place in the South Harbour of Copenhagen

Collaborative values of illutron

Cultural eventspace

It is floating culture house, workshop and event platform for installation art, music, fire and light.
