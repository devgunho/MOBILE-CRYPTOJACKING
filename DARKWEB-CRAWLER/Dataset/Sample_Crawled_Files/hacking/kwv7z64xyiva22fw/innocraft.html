Funnels

Identify and understand where your visitors drop off

A Funnel defines a series of actions that you expect your visitors to take on their way to converting a goal. Funnels visualizes these actions so you can see how well they succeed at each step. Based on this information you can improve your website or app to optimize your conversion rates and sales.

Learn more

Users Flow

Learn how your users navigate through your website or app

Users Flow shows you the most popular paths your users took through your website or app. In a glance you see how your users navigate through your website over several steps, where they came from and where they exit.
