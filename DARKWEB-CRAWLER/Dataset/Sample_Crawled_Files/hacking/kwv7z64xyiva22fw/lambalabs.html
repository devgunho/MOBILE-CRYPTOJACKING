Lamba Labs is all about bringing in the #community of like minded makers together. One of our ongoing support goes to CodeBrave, a collective of volunteers and educators who developed a #maker centric #education curriculum for Home of Hope. This foster home houses kids from various Arab nationalities who are stuck in the judicial system. Some even don't have ID forms, which means they cannot enroll in a school (public or otherwise) nor have the chance for gainful employment once they grow up. Since the local government has no plans to issue them these IDs, CodeBrave stepped in and started a school just for them. They integrated maker skills and programming into their currciculum to give them a fighting chance by taking on freelance projects.
