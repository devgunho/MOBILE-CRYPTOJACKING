Reasonably priced, conveniently located and close to nature

 

Already from the exterior, the clear design signature of the Motel Verde's architecture is evident. Rooms from € 44,-- per night and room. Lots of wood, stone and green enable the building to perfectly blend into the natural surroundings. The inside of the motel Verde also has much to offer. Natural materials create a pleasant atmosphere. In all of the details, greatest emphasis was placed on appealing design.
For a quick bite and for breakfast, there are snacks and drinks from the vending machine.



 

Room facilities

The perfectly equipped rooms leave nothing to be desired and have ultra-modern facilities with design furniture, flatscreen LED TV, double beds that can also be split into two single beds, fully equipped bathrooms, terrace or balcony and wireless Internet access.

Choose your room from 42 individual air-conditioned rooms and our categories, either on the ground floor with private terrace and separable beds, on the second floor with balcony and double bed, or on the third floor with a double bed without balcony.


 

  • Rooms with private terrace or balcony
  • LED TV with a wide selection of TV programs
  • Wireless Internet access
  • Safe (including for laptops)
  • Anti-allergy sleeping materials
  • Separate toilet and bathroom
  • Heated towel rail
  • Hairdryer and cosmetic mirror
