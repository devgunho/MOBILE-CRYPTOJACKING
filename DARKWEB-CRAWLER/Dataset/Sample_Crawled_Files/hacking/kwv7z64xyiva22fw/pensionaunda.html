Pension a&a

Located in a pedestrian area in the historic center of Vienna, our stylish guest house with contemporary design is only a 3-minute walk from Saint Stephen's Cathedral and the Stephansplatz Underground Station (lines U1 and U3).

A typical Viennese buffet breakfast and fresh newspapers are offered every morning from 08.00 till 11.00. Free WiFi is available throughout the pension.

The modern, air-conditioned rooms at feature a flat-screen TV, a tea kettle, a laptop-size safe and a bathroom with a hairdryer.
