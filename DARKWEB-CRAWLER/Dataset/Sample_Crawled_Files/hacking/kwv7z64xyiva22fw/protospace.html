We make space for you to make things

Protospace is Calgary's original, community-based, member-driven maker space, offering access to work shop space, education, community, and shared tools for your projects.

We exist to facilitate member projects and interests by providing a physical space, tools, and a friendly, welcoming community of makers. Members get 24/7 access to the space and it's resources, so join us now.

Our space is located in the Northeast quadrant of the city, and our doors are open to the public every Tuesday night at 7pm. Come on out to a Meet and Geek, check out our member events and projects around this website, or feel free toÂ contact us if you have any questions!
