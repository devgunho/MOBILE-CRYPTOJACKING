Welcome to Hotel & Palais Strudlhof

Hotel & Palais Strudlhof, a traditional 4-star hotel in Vienna’s ninth district, is located in a quiet area just a short walk away from the charming city center. Business travelers appreciate the convenient access to public transportation, while holiday guests use our hotel as an optimal base for exploring Vienna and enjoy the extras it offers. Surrounded by the atmosphere and architecture of the Austro-Hungarian Empire, Hotel & Palais Strudlhof elegantly combines history and tradition with modernity.
