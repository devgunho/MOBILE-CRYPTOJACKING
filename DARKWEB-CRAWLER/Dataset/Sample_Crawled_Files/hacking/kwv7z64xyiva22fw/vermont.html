May 24, 2018

If every Vermonter affected by one of the most recent breaches took advantage of a credit freeze and lifted a freeze at each of the major credit reporting agencies it would represent more than $10 million of savings to Vermonters.   • Sunlight and Transparency. Providing consumers with information about commercial entities with whom they have no direct relationship and what options they may have to opt out of certain lists or offerings provides transparency, provides them real options when or if a data breach occurs that may affect them, and provides consumers and regulators with important information about a growing industry.

New revelations about data breaches and exposure of consumer information from major companies has become almost routine.
