Where are we located ?

Our main lodge is located on the north shore of the Taureau lake, 15 miles north of St-Michel-des-Saints, 115 miles north of Montreal. Located on a pristine hunting territory, between 2 Provincial Parks, the lodge is easily accessible by road, only a day’s drive from New England and the northeast U.S.

For many years, CanadAventure’s main lodge has been referred as « the most comfortable hunting camp » in Eastern Canada !
