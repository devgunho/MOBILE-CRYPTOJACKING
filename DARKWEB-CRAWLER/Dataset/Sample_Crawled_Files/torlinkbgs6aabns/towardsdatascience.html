The above algorithms are tools that could potentially be useful for detecting attacks if used in the right way. Aside from the challenges mentioned already, there are some other significant ingredients that we are missing. The first one is context. In addition to context, we need to build systems with expert knowledge. Ideally systems that help us capture expert knowledge in simple ways.
