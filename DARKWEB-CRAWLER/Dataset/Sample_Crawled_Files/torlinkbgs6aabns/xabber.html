About developers

Xabber was originally developed by Redsolution, Inc. In early 2013, Xabber was licensed under GNU/GPL v.3 license and quickly became the most popular XMPP client for Android. Since then, a number of individuals joined Xabber as developers, testers and translators.

Our goal is to create a stable, reliable and user friendly ecosystem for instant messaging that does not rely on proprietary services. We welcome anyone who believes in open standards and free information interchange to take part in moving Xabber forward.
